5 Fashion Tips to Ensure You Always Look Stylish

Jun 7, 2023 • 2 min read

Become your own master stylist with a few reliable tips.

1. Work your capsule wardrobe: Invest in reliable wardrobe staples that can be mixed and matched, such as a little black dress, well-fitting jeans, a classic blazer, neutral-colored T-shirts and button-downs, and a versatile leather or denim jacket.

2. Ensure perfect fit: Consider hiring a good tailor to make adjustments to your clothing. Well-fitted clothes not only look polished but also feel more comfortable. When your capsule wardrobe fits well, you can experiment with oversized or undersized items without compromising style.

3. Balance proportions: Create aesthetic harmony in your outfits by balancing proportions. Wear clothes that are fitted to your body shape. When incorporating oversized or unconventional shapes, pair them with fitted pieces to create a fashionable look. For example, pair a tight crop top with wide-leg jeans or a puff-shoulder top with straight-leg pants.

4. Find your personal style: Take the time to explore and develop your own signature style. Create a moodboard for inspiration and experiment with different colors, shapes, and silhouettes. Don't limit yourself to traditional gendered categories and explore what looks great on your unique body.

5. Become a better shopper: Learn how to shop for items that you truly love and will wear frequently. Avoid filling your closet with items that will go unworn. By curating a wardrobe of pieces you adore, styling outfits will become more intuitive.

Women Fashion Guide: Why it Matters About What to Wear

Jun 21, 2023 • 2 min read

Take it from the experts

As a woman, it is crucial to understand the impact of what you wear. Your clothing choices define your style and influence how others perceive you. While some women are content with a t-shirt and jeans, it's important to be conscious of your body shape and dress in a way that makes you feel confident. The clothes you wear affect how you speak, walk, interact, and even smile, so it's essential to choose garments that make you happy and comfortable.

Your clothes should reflect your personal style and bring you joy. Wearing something that makes you feel great can have a positive impact on your self-esteem. When you feel good in your outfit, it affects your personal and professional life, as well as the people around you. It's crucial to dress with confidence and respect, as it shows consideration for others and enhances your own self-worth.

Learning about fashion and finding your personal dressing code shouldn't be overwhelming. Dressing well is a skill that anyone can develop. Experiment with different styles, colors, and shapes to discover what suits you best. The process may feel daunting at first, but with practice, you'll become more comfortable in choosing the right outfits that make you feel confident.

Find what works for you and stick with it. Identify colors that complement your complexion and create a balanced wardrobe. Choose clothes that make you comfortable and avoid purchasing items that don't align with your personal style. Your closet should be filled with garments that make you feel confident and happy.

Remember, dressing well is not about following strict rules, but rather about expressing your unique self. Embrace your personal style, wear clothes that make you feel great, and radiate confidence in everything you do. By prioritizing your own comfort and happiness, you'll exude a sense of authenticity that will positively impact your life and interactions with others.
